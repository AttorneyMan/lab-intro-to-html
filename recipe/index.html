HOT AND SPICY LOADED NACHOS

For game day or “just because”, this zesty palate pleaser is a must-try appetizer. Mix up melty cheese and Franks RedHot® Queso Dip Seasoning Mix and drizzle over cooked ground beef and crispy tortilla chips. Top with the traditional nacho fixins and get ready for some big flavor.

    INGREDIENTS

  • 1 can (14 1/2 ounces) petite diced tomatoes, drained
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 package Frank's RedHot® Queso Dip Seasoning Mix
  • 1 package (16 ounces) prepared cheese product such as Velveeta®, cubed
  • 1 package (13 ounces) tortilla chips
  • 1 pound lean ground beef, cooked and crumbled

DIRECTIONS

  • PREHEAT oven to 350°F. Mix tomatoes, milk, Seasoning Mix, and cheese in large microwavable bowl. Microwave on HIGH about 4 minutes, stirring halfway through, until cheese is melted and smooth.
  • ARRANGE tortilla chips in single layer on lightly greased foil-lined shallow baking pan, overlapping chips if needed to cover bottom completely. Top chips with beef and drizzle with cheese sauce.
  • BAKE 10 minutes. Top with Bacon , chopped avocado, onion and cilantro, if desired.
